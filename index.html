Hey, I'm Jack Halloran — a freshman at Xavier University studying Cybersecurity. Before I even stepped foot on campus, I knew I wanted to pursue something in tech. The field just made sense to me: it's always evolving, always challenging, and it rewards curiosity.

When Xavier launched Cybersecurity as a brand-new major, it felt like a perfect fit. It checked both boxes — a passion for technology and a love of continuous learning.
